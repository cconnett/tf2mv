Privacy Policy

By joining this group, you give TF2MV permission to:

  • Poll your TF2 backpack at regular intervals using the Steam Web API.
  • Store the data on servers in the United States.
  • Publish non-personally identifiable aggregate data derived from the backpack data gathered.

You may revoke this permission at any time by leaving the group. If you do so, we will:

  • Stop polling your TF2 backpack.
  • Delete the backpack data we have collected about your Steam account in a timely manner.

If your backpack data was used in the creation of derived or aggregate data, the derived data will survive and continue to be made available.
